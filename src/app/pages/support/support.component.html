1 Purpose:

     To regulate GJS's service terms and standards for Indonesian after-sales returns and repairs to further improve the quality and level of service of after-sales services.

2 Scope:

     Applies to service management of after-sales returns, replacements, and repairs for GJS Robots Indonesia distributors, dealers, and end-user.

3 Definitions:

     DOA (Dead on Arrival), refers to goods that are defective on arrival. After receiving goods from GJS Robots Indonesia, to find that the goods appear to be damaged or have performance failure. Once such conditions are confirmed, it is entitled to apply for a replacement.

     DAP (Dead after Purchase), refers to machines that are found to fall within the scope of performance failure within 30 days of purchase by end-users. The end-user can apply for a parts replacement and must provide valid proof of purchase, which includes but is not limited to machine invoices, warranty cards, sales receipts, etc.

     In Warranty, refers to the discovery of non-human performance failure from the date of purchase by the end-user and within the warranty period. The end-user is entitled to repair services at an authorized GJS Robots Indonesia distributor with valid proof, which includes but is not limited to machine invoices, warranty cards, sales receipts, etc.

     Beyond Warranty, refers to the period after purchase by the end-user beyond the warranty period or the discovery of human performance failure. The end-user is entitled to paid repair services at an authorized GJS Robots Indonesia distributor.

5 Paid repairs: Under the following conditions, a reasonable fee must be charged

(1) Out-of-warranty;

(2) No proof of purchase and valid invoice (except for proof of the warranty period);

(3) The contents of the purchase certificate or the logo of the physical product does not match or is altered;

(4) Product use or repair is inconsistent with the instructions for product use in the manual, or damage is caused;

(5) Damage caused by unauthorized dismantling or repair, unauthorized repair or modification by a company different from GJS Robots Indonesia or Distributors;

(6) Wetness, moisture, mildew;

(7) Failure of equipment accessories and other components aside from the main components listed above;

(8) Damage due to force majeure.

6 Supplementary information: The above-mentioned after-sales service policy is subject to change.
